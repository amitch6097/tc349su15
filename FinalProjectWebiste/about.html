A B O U T
M I S S I O N

The Wasatch project is for dedicated intermediate to advanced freeskiers and snowboarders trying to reach their maximum potential. The Wasatch Project teaches character development, mountain safety, and the most progressive freeskiing and snowboarding techniques, all while having fun! Wether you're trying to make it to the next level by traveling and filming, or you have a strong passion for competition, this program has the steps and opportunities to get you there. We have a range of programs available to meet the needs of each athlete.

C O M P A N Y / O V E R V I E W

The Wasatch Project is a freestyle community designed to assist up-and-coming freeskiers and snowboarders by giving them every possible opportunity to succeed in what they love. By combining our efforts, we can all bring costs of traveling and competing down. We create a resource for fundraising and opportunities for success.
